Special Events

Super Smash Revolution

Super Smash Revolution is our largest event of the year where we host tournaments for numerous titles oriented around rhythm and fighting game themes.

Originally started in 2019, Super Smash Revolution (SSR) has been a hit every year as we continue to grow the event and increase in participants

In the past the following games have been seen at SSR:

  • Beat Saber
  • Clone Hero
  • Dance Dance Revolution
  • Guilty Gear Strive
  • Guitar Hero
  • Smash Bros Melee
  • Smash Bros Ultimate

SalukiCon

Working with the university we host several tournaments during our campus ComicCon known as SalukiCon.

Games we have hosted for SalukiCon in the past have included:

  • Dance Dance Revolution
  • League of Legends
  • Rocket League
  • Smash Bros Ultimate

Besides video game based tournaments the multiday event include much more such as table top games, comic and pop culture related vendors, food vendors, and cosplay competitions.

SIU Showdown

Starting in 2021, with our sister school, we began a tournament known as SIU Showdown where SIU Carbondale and SIU Edwardsville compete for the pride of knowing who is the better competitive esports program that year.

Titles that have competed before include:

  • League of Legends
  • Overwatch
  • Rainbow Six Siege
  • Smash Bros Ultimate
  • Valorant
