SIU ESports

What is SIU ESports?

SIU ESports is our program for collegiate gaming where we strive to compete at the highest level. With over 10 competitive teams we aim to push higher to improve our teams, players, and program.

Competitive
And
Casual

At SIU we provide a wide range of games to compete in such as Rocket League, Valorant, CS:GO, and more. Every week these teams are practing to not only compete but to win.


If the competitive scene is not for you, we offer a variety of casual games including Dance Dance Revolution, Guilty Gear Strive, Smash Bros, and more.

If you are interested in joining one of our teams or meeting with other casual gamers, use the button below to join our discord for more information.
