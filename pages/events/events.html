Events

Annual Events

Super Smash Revolution

Every year we host Super Smash Revolution, a tournament with multiple rhythm based and fighting game oriented titles.

It draws in participants from numerous surrounding colleges and cities with it being open for anybody to participate in.

SalukiCon

In conjunction with the university we host various tournaments during the Con.

In the past titles we've hosted include Dance Dance Revolution, Smash Bros Ultimate, and Rocket League.

SIU Showdown

Starting in 2021, together with SIUE, we starting an annual competition known as SIU Showdown.

In this tournament, multiple of our competitive esports titles compete for pride in knowing who is the better sister school in Esports.
