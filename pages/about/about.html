About Us

Our History

Originally we were founded in 2016 by a group of students looking to form a club for collegiate gaming.

Together with the already existing Club Rhythm, we formed a new group known as SIU ESports.

In 2018, a project to built a public community Esports Arena in the Student Center began to develop led by the late Chancellor Carlo Montemagno.

Then by the summer of 2019 it was complete, with the opening ceremony taking place on August 16th.

Since then SIU ESports has been been competiting 8 to 11 collegiate esport titles in leagues such as the National Esports Collegiate Conference (NECC), Collegiate Cod League (CCL), FACEIT, Apex Spark Series, and more.

The SIU ESports
Mission

At SIU we are always pushing and exploring new opportunities to improve our play and our program.

Every week, semester, and year aim for higher performance and results. In parallel, we seek to grown and maintain a strong casual gamer following.
