Rhythm

Rhythm Games are one of our oldest games in Esports! Rhythm meets in the arena multiple times a week to enjoy our most physical games.

Common titles that Rhythm plays include:

  • Dance Dance Revolution (Stepmania)
  • Clone Hero/Guitar Hero
  • Beat Saber
  • Osu!
  • And more!

Be sure to check out our Weeklies page to see Rhythm's meeting times!
